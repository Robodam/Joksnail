Joks Snail farm is an agricultural firm, registered under the Corporate Affairs Commission.

Research shows that snail meats possess anti-cancer properties and boost the immune system due to their antioxidants and anti-inflammatory effects. An average snail is comprised of 80% water, 15% protein and 2.4% fat. They contain essential fatty acids, calcium, iron, selenium, magnesium and are a rich source of vitamins E, A, K and B12 which are vital for healthy living.

  • It is in a bid to make snail meat affordable and available at all time to every class of citizens that Joks snail farm was birthed. Also, the quest to provide cancer prevention,discover nature and watch things come to life was a key motivation to start the business.
  • In our 2years of operations, we have supplied over 4,000 piece of snails to over 7 states in Nigeria. We have helped over 5 snail farmers to start up their own snail farms.
    • In an effort to encourage youths to embrace snail farming, we have organised online tranings in which over 65 youths benefitted from. Some of them are already practicing snail farmers.

FAQ

FAQ

Informations you need to know about our Giant Snail in Jokssnailfarm.

  • Why does snails eat it own shell?

    The reason your snails eat it's shell is because it lack calcium.

  • Can snail rebuild it's shell when damaged?

    Yes, just ensure you give the snail enough calcium.

  • Is it true that snails can be both male and female at the same time?

    Yes, African giant snails are hermaphrodites.

  • How do snails produce partners on their shell?

    Ans, partners and design on shell are produced naturally by the animal that makes the shell and the partner is actually part of the shell.

  • How long can a snail live?

    Ans African giant snails can live for over 5 years and more provided they are well taken care of.

  • How can you tell if a snail is sexually matured?

    Snails which develop aperture, teeth or reflected lips are sexually matured

  • Does self fertilization occur?

    Cross fertilization is the rule ,but self fertilization does occur rarely.

  • Do all species lay eggs?

    Only one species called Achatina iradelei gives birth to live young snails, though the babies are born from eggs but these eggs are incubated inside the snail.

  • Does snails have personality?

    Yes, some snails are greedy and heavy eaters.

  • What is the largest species?

    The largest species are the archachatina Marginata and Achatina Achatina.

  • How long does it take a baby snail to grow to large snails?

    18months.

  • Where can I sell my snails?

    Restaurant, hotels, food vendors and social media platforms?
